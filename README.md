## Hello, strangers!

Welcome to my little library, where thoughts are woven into lines of code, and imagination slowly learns how to breathe through machines.

I am a developer wandering between the worlds of Game Development, Artificial Intelligence, and Web Development — chasing the beauty of turning impossible ideas into something real, interactive, and alive.

Honestly, I am simply someone who fell in love with the magic of programming; how mere instructions whispered to a machine can create worlds, tell stories, solve problems, and sometimes even make dreams tangible.
